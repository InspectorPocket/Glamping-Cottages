Cottages

Alder, Willow, Oak

Open Plan Cottages - Fully Equipped Kitchens - Private Bathrooms - Sleeps Up To Four

  •

The cottages feature a fully equipped kitchen, and have a private garden area to the front with outdoor seating.

Alder, Willow and Oak are available in two different bed configurations:
Three Single Beds OR One Double Bed and One Single
Both options are accompanied with a sofa bed in the lounge.

Dogs are welcome as long as a bed or basket is provided for them, as they are not allowed on the furniture.
