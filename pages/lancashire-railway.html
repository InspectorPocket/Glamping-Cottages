East Lancashire Railway

  • Written by: John Vaughan
  • Leave your thoughts →

We're very lucky to be so close to one of the few remaining steam train services in England.

Rawtenstall train station is only a 15 minute drive from our cottages. With stops at Irwell Vale, Ramsbottom, Summerseat and Bury.

They have many events on throughout the year. The children will love their days with Thomas and the very festive Santa Special. For adults they host fabulous dining experiences, murder mystery nights, or for the child in all of us you can become a train driver for the day!

To keep up to date with all the current events on at the railway check out their website.

www.eastlancsrailway.org.uk
