Cycling

  • Last activity took place on: 26th January 2017
  • Leave your thoughts →

Expect to rack up plently of miles on Strava! Our site at Rossendale Holidays is surrounded by brilliant road and mountain biking routes. Lee Quarry is a must for any keen mountain bikers, with various routes for people of different levels.

We highly recommend the Panoptican Tour, a route planned by our friend David, a NSIQ Cycling tutor, and a British Cycling Ride Leader. Take a tour of Haslingden Halo, The Atom and The Singing Ringing Tree, or even Colourfields in Blackburn, if you can manage that far!

Up for a challenge? 5 miles? 10? 100? As a route planner and risk assesor, David will cater to any biking routes, road or mountain, so if you have anywhere in mind, let us know!

Walking

  • Last activity took place on: 26th January 2017
  • Leave your thoughts →

The Rossendale Valley has a long history for walkers and hikers. The village of Water and the surrounding areas are stunning and serene. As the name might suggest, there is an abundance of rivers, streams and reservoirs.

Explore the area with miles of natural and man-made trails.

Walk on the national trails. With many close routes you can explore England's finest countryside, see spectacular views and return to your comfortable beds at the end of the day.

The Island

  • Last activity took place on: 26th January 2017
  • Leave your thoughts →

Follow the steps down to "The Island", with its babbling brook and weeping willows around the border, it is the perfect retreat at the end of the day. While away spend those summer days with friends and family around the BBQ or huddle round the stone fire pit for night time campfires.
